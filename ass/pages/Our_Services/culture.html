Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide bilingual or regional language pandits?

Yes, we have certified pandits who are fluent in Hindi, English, Gujarati, Sanskrit, and other regional languages to ensure you understand every aspect of the ceremony.

Can I customize the ceremony based on family traditions?

Absolutely! We work closely with families to incorporate specific traditions, customs, and preferences while maintaining the spiritual authenticity of the ceremony.

Do you conduct eco-friendly havans?

Yes, we offer eco-friendly havan options using natural, biodegradable materials while preserving the traditional sanctity of the fire ceremony.

Is online/live-streamed ritual support available?

Yes, we provide live streaming services for family members who cannot attend in person, ensuring everyone can participate in the sacred ceremony.

How do we book a specific date or Muhurat?

Our pandits can help determine auspicious dates and timings (muhurat) according to traditional calculations, or we can work with dates you've already selected.

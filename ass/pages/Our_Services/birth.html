Frequently Asked Questions

How early should we book a party? +

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability of your preferred date and theme. For popular seasons or special themes, earlier booking is advised.

Do you offer indoor and outdoor events? +

Yes! We specialize in both indoor and outdoor celebrations. We'll help you choose the perfect venue and adapt our decorations and entertainment to suit your space.

What's the age range you cover? +

We plan parties for all ages - from 1st birthday celebrations to milestone adult parties. Our themes and entertainment are customized based on the age group and preferences.

Are entertainers included? +

Entertainment options are included in most of our packages. This can include magicians, face painters, puppet shows, DJs, or other age-appropriate entertainers based on your theme.

Can we customize the theme fully? +

Absolutely! While we have popular themes available, we love creating completely custom experiences. Share your vision with us and we'll bring it to life with our creative expertise.
